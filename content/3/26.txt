<p> 
lambkins<br>                               
left them alone<br>    
nowhere and none<br>    
housed to kill<br>    
hair trimmed<br>    
abdomens ripped<br>    
they graze<br>    
modern people do<br>    
not eat them do not<br>    
like killing do not kill<br>    
anymore<br>    
</p>
