<p> 
knock knock hens<br>                
have you any eggs?<br>    
yes sir a child<br>    
enough for breakfast<br>    
knock knock cows<br>    
have you any milk<br>    
yes sir steal<br>    
what remains<br>    
knock knock bees<br>    
honey? yes sir<br>    
we are on the spoon<br>    
</p>
