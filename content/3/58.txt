"author": "Kinga Tóth",
"type" : "poem"
}
===
<p> 
mother calls<br>    
only four<br>    
come back<br>    
another day<br>    
another call<br>    
just three just two<br>    
come home 1<br>    
and that’s<br>    
where they gather<br>    
where they escape<br>    
new ones are laid<br>
</p>
