<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">3</h3>
<p>
good morning<br>
MrPumpkin<br>
rise your mouth<br>
will be cut<br>
for breath<br>
</p>
<p>
the head is gut<br>
no burning inside<br>
they dry it before<br>
your morning<br>
on lantern day<br>
yippiyee<br>
</p>

<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">4</h3>
<p>
drawing<br>
from the lamp hangs sister<br>
swings frightens mother<br>
in the pot dumplings drop<br>
blue the kitchen furniture<br>
the table we cannot reach<br>
even from the chair<br>
she leaps from the lamp<br>
</p>

<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">8</h3>
<p>
pig on a plank<br>
right and left<br>
brown ground<br>
airless space<br>
no animals <br>
moving<br>
</p>

<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">10</h3>
<p>
stomach<br>
in three parts<br>                
the biggest a bear<br>
prepares and eats<br>
in a chamber<br>
the smallest sleeps<br>
on a bunk bed<br>
the last the library<br>
with coloring books<br>
and the walls leak<br>
slowly<br>
</p>

<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">11</h3>
<p>
broadside<br>
under water<br> 
seagulls hear<br>
hungry passengers<br> 
shrivelled like grain<br>
what do seagulls want<br>
</p>

<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">14</h3>
<p> 
london burning<br>                                                                            
burning call<br>                                               
firemen engineer<br>                                             
bring water fire fire<br>                                               
london is burning<br>                                               
pest is burning<br>    
</p>

<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">15</h3>
<p> 
oak<br>     
bean grow<br>                        
farmer<br>     
sets plants<br>    
into the ground<br>     
into the hole<br>

something might grow<br>    
</p>

<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">19</h3>
<p> 
brush your teeth<br>                                              
they rot like uncle’s<br>    
the crocodile clock rings<br>    
the holder vibrates<br>    
</p>

<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">20</h3>
<p> 
small fish in the lake<br>                                                 
mom fries them in oil<br>    
dad ate them all<br>    
now he is the man<br>    
</p>

<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">26</h3>
<p> 
lambkins<br>                               
left them alone<br>    
nowhere and none<br>    
housed to kill<br>    
hair trimmed<br>    
abdomens ripped<br>    
they graze<br>    
modern people do<br>    
not eat them do not<br>    
like killing do not kill<br>    
anymore<br>    
</p>

<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">33</h3>
<p> 
1: mouse<br>    
2: cat<br>    
mouse the center cat outside<br>    
we are the circle and watch<br>    
as he tears it we all watch<br>    
</p>

</p>

<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">45</h3>
<p>

he touches me<br>

for stress and stabs<br>

<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">48</h3>
<p> 
red the fireman burns<br>    
orange the tiger<br>    
yellow the sun reaches the skin<br>    
green the christmas tree in winter<br>    
alone and before shrivelling<br>    
incomplete and purple<br>    
the humans<br>    
</p>

<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">54</h3>
<p>

march upwards<br>    
rain starts<br>    
chases them down<br>    
tumbler glints nicely<br>    
sun spouts sparks<br>    
</p>

<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">57</h3>
<p>

have you any eggs?<br>    
yes sir a child<br>    
enough for breakfast<br>    
knock knock cows<br>    
have you any milk<br>    
yes sir steal<br>    
what remains<br>    
knock knock bees<br>    
honey? yes sir<br>    
we are on the spoon<br>    
</p>

<h3 style="font-size: 22px;">58</h3>
<p>

only four<br>    
come back<br>

come home 1<br>

</p>
