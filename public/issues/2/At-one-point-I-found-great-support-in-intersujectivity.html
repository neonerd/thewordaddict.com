    At one point, I found great support in ‘intersujectivity’.

    by Ian Mykiska

    At one point, I found great support in ‘intersujectivity’.
    - Knowledge of isolation and desperately incommunicable experience
       takes on a different shade in light of event/connection/relationship
         - the bigger the gates of determination
         the less does not remain to be communicated
             and under the bluelight veil
             of intuitive good
    (joy is also intuitive)
             everything makes sense
             and has sense.
               “if at least every hundredth line quivers every hundredth heart”
    – Oldřich Mikulášek, Czech poet.

    .

    ..

    ...

    .../
    every hundredth can’t always be enough, well, sometimes, at least
    but with the world’s imperfections the problem has to be in me
    So tell me, what the fuck should I deconstruct
    and how far do we have to go!
